Florida Itenerary

March 19 (Wednesday): Arrival and Relaxation

  • Morning/Afternoon:
    • Arrive in Florida (Clearwater or nearby city).
    • Check into your accommodation and settle in.

  • Evening:
    • Enjoy a relaxing dinner at a local restaurant
    • Take a short evening walk or explore nearby attractions.

March 20 (Thursday): Universal Studios

  • Morning:
    • Drive to Universal Orlando Resort (2-hour drive from Clearwater).
    • Start your day early to make the most of the park.
    • Visit popular rides like Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey and VelociCoaster.

  • Afternoon:
    • Take a break for lunch at Three Broomsticks or CityWalk.
    • Explore more attractions in Universal Studios Florida.

  • Evening:
    • Stay for nighttime shows like Cinematic Celebration before heading back.

March 21 (Friday): Busch Gardens Tampa Bay

  • Morning:
    • Head to Busch Gardens (30-40 minutes from Clearwater).
    • Enjoy thrilling coasters like Iron Gwazi and Cheetah Hunt.

  • Afternoon:
    • Explore the Serengeti Safari or animal exhibits.
    • Take a break for lunch at Zambia Smokehouse.

  • Evening:
    • Wind down with evening rides or entertainment before heading back.

March 22 (Saturday): Pickleball and Local Fun

  • Morning:
    • Play pickleball at a local court like Countryside Country Club or a public park in Clearwater.
    • Engage in a friendly match or join a local group.

  • Afternoon:
    • Explore Downtown Dunedin, known for its breweries and shops.
    • Optional: Visit Caladesi Island (accessible by ferry, no swimming required).

  • Evening:
    • Have dinner
